Who We Are

Founded in 2024 by a pair of MIT Ph.D graduates, Stelluvia Technologies offers innovative solutions that leverage artificial intelligence, advanced data analysis and statistical techniques. We take pride in developing customized solutions that meet the unique needs of each client, ensuring exceptional results tailored to their specific challenges. Drawing on our backgrounds in aeronautical engineering and space sciences, we specialize in advancing space exploration and technology, driving groundbreaking innovations that push forward the future of space.

Our Vision

AI is taking an increasingly prominent role in our world, but despite its name, it lacks true intelligence. We believe that AI excels at performing complex calculations but requires human oversight to ensure its effectiveness. At Stelluvia Technologies, we envision a "cyborg" future where AI handles data analysis with its speed and precision, while humans provide the essential judgment for informed decision-making. We believe this model, with AI agents operating under human handlers, will maximize efficiency and drive innovation by combining the strengths of both technology and human insight.

Our Core Capabilities

Artificial Intelligence

AI is at the core of everything we do at Stelluvia. Our expertise ranges from advanced data analytics to implementing automated solutions to reducing cognitive load by optimizing real-time decision making systems. Explore our other capabilities to discover what we're doing with AI.

Computer Vision

We have extensive experience in developing and applying sophisticated algorithms to interpret visual data. From cellphone photos to high-resolution spectrographs, Stelluvia offers cutting-edge solutions for all your computer vision challenges.

Advanced Analytics

We employ cutting-edge statistical methods to dive deep into complex datasets, uncovering valuable insights from even the largest datasets. From predictive modeling to data visualization, our advanced analytics techniques can be trusted to find the needle in your haystack.

Edge Computing

Stelluvia specializes in implementing cutting edge software solutions on flight-ready hardware. In the space domain and closer to home, our edge-computing methods are designed to reduce latency, minimize transmission bandwidth, and maximize decision making time.

Remote Sensing

From high resolution hyper-spectral imaging to radar systems, Stelluvia excels in leveraging remote sensing to gather and analyze data from afar. With Stelluvia AI agents on the edge feeding high-priority information to human handlers, we ensure precise insights are delivered quickly and efficiently.

Satellite Operations

Stelluvia excels in satellite operations, with expertise in designing, building, launching, and operating satellite constellations as well as in-house ground stations. We have also implemented automated satellite constellation management processes for nominal operation as well as collision avoidance to reduce operational load on ground controllers.

Atmospheric Sciences

Stelluvia Technologies harnesses sophisticated modeling and simulation techniques to advance our understanding of atmospheric phenomena. We have expertise in GCMs, aerosol modelling, atmospheric dynamics and in particular radiative transfer.

Fluid Mechanics

Stelluvia Technologies excels in using high performance computing to perform advanced CFD simulations. Our expertise encompasses a range of complex fluid setups, including chemical reactions, radiative processes, and multi-fluid interactions, as well as aerodynamic modeling using finite element methods. Using Physics Informed Neural Networks, we are able to run multi-scale calculations quickly and efficiently.
